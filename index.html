Company Profile

SS Industrial Solution is a professionally managed industrial service company established in 2020. Backed by more than 10 years of hands-on experience in the electro-mechanical field, we provide reliable, safe and cost-effective solutions for industrial clients. We specialize in installation, maintenance, repair, commissioning and project execution services for pumps, electrical systems, industrial pipelines, EOT cranes, industrial equipment and plant utilities.
